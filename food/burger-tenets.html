What is a burger?

  • 1. A burger must contain a patty between at least two pieces of leavened bread.
  • 2. The patty does not have to be meat based.
  • 3. The burger must contain at least 1 condiment.
  • 4. A condiment is defined as a sauce or pickled vegetable.
  • 5. The patty must weigh at least 2 oz uncooked.
